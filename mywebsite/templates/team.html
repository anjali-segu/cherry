Anjali Segu is currently a senior at Westwood High School. She is apart of the 4 year computer science track at her school. She has also completed 200+ hours of training on Harvard CS50, Udemy, and Codecademy. She has been actively volunteering at local Austin charities for the past 6 years.

''

Over the past few years my experiences with volunteering at local charities has led me to realize how prohibitively expensive developing and maintaining a modern website is for any small organization. At that moment I knew I had to engineer better technology to support these small charity organizations. Over my senior and junior I dedicated myself to sign up for almost every technology online class I could find. And soon CherryGiver was born.

CherryGiver allows charities to be given the opportunity to explain the work they do. So many charities go unheard of every day simply because they don't have the technology. My goal over the next few years is to reach out to more and more charities and give them the chance to have a platform on which they can clearly explain what they work on.
